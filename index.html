How to Play

Game Objective

The goal of Smarty Bubbles is to clear all the colored bubbles from the screen by matching three or more bubbles of the same color. Shoot bubbles strategically to create chains and clear the board.

Controls

  • Use your mouse or touchscreen to aim
  • Click or tap to shoot the bubble
  • Match three or more bubbles of the same color to pop them
  • Create chain reactions for bonus points
  • Clear all bubbles before they reach the bottom line

Special Bubbles

Look out for special power-up bubbles that can help you clear the board:

  • Bomb Bubbles - Explode and clear surrounding bubbles
  • Rainbow Bubbles - Match with any color
  • Lightning Bubbles - Clear entire rows
  • Star Bubbles - Clear all bubbles of one color

Scoring

Points are awarded based on:

  • Number of bubbles popped in one shot
  • Chain reaction effects
  • Remaining bubbles at level completion
  • Speed of level completion
